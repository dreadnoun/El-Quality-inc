Our Services

EL Quality Inc offers a wide range of transportation services across all states and potentially Canada. We are committed to providing reliable and efficient logistics solutions.

Cargo Types We Handle

We specialize primarily in Dry Van and Drop & Hook services, including flatbed cargo. However, our capabilities extend to various other types, such as Reefer, Hazmat, Step Deck, and more. We handle diverse freight, including Amazon loads, various dedicated lines, and mail.

Market Position & Partnerships

We maintain a competitive edge in the market through efficient operations and strong relationships. We work with experienced brokers and hold contracts with major players like Amazon, JB Hunt, Schneider, TQL, ensuring consistent and valuable freight opportunities.

Operational Support

We utilize a reliable wire payment system and offer advance payment options upon completion of work. For owner-operators, we can also assist with providing license plates (details to be discussed).

Driver Opportunities

We are currently seeking experienced owner-operators (minimum 2 years of experience). We also consider company drivers who demonstrate excellent performance and reliability. Join our team and benefit from our supportive environment and strong industry connections.
